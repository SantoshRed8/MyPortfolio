Do you provide hosting services?

While I don't provide hosting directly, I can recommend reliable hosting providers based on your needs and budget. I'll also handle the setup and deployment of your website on your chosen hosting platform.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

Absolutely! Depending on your technical comfort level, I can implement a user-friendly content management system (CMS) that allows you to make updates without any coding knowledge. I also provide training to ensure you're comfortable managing your site.

What technologies do you use?

Yes, I include a set number of revisions in every project package to ensure the final product meets your expectations. I encourage feedback at each stage of development so we can make adjustments along the way. Additional revisions beyond the included scope can also be arranged if needed.
